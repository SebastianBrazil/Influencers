Their Influence On Me

Why is Brenda influential to me? Well, I believe it's natural for my best friend to be an influential person to me. She was by my side when I was at my lowest, helping me pull on through. I stand by her side when she goes through her problems. She gives me support, advice, and in general someone to talk to about personal topics. She's my inspiration be become a better person, and she's told me that I'm her inspiration too. I owe so much to her and I'm honored to be able to help her in her life just like she helped me. Brenda, thank you for everything.
